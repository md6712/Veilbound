VEIL ATTUNEMENT RANKS – VEILBOUND WORLD

🌫️ OVERVIEW
Not all who feel the Veil can wield it. The path of magical development in the Veilbound world is gradual, treacherous, and deeply personal. The following guide describes the general progression of attunement to the Veil, from the first signs of resonance to the formal title of Mage.

These ranks are recognized by most academies and the Council of the Veil. However, progression is not guaranteed — many stall or burn out before reaching mastery.

==============================
🔹 1. VEILTOUCHED
==============================
- Description: The first stage of awareness. The person has a Veilseed — a dormant magical potential — but has not manifested an Affinity.
- Signs: Strange dreams, emotional sensitivity, minor elemental reactions (e.g., warmth in the presence of fire).
- Age Range: Typically from birth to early adolescence.
- Risks: None, but early affinity tests (if conducted) are vague and often politically influenced.

==============================
🔹 2. VEILMARKED
==============================
- Description: Affinity has manifested, often triggered by trauma, emotion, or powerful experiences.
- Signs: Clear elemental or emotional reactions, spells cast unconsciously or instinctively.
- Status: Legally recognized as a magic-user. Must register with the local Veil registry or temple.
- Limitation: Still lacks control, stamina, and formal understanding.

==============================
🔹 3. VEILBOUND
==============================
- Description: Has bonded with their Affinity and can call upon it intentionally. Often receives formal training.
- Signs: Can perform basic to moderate spells using their Affinity, understands Veil safety.
- Training: Typically trains under an experienced mage, council guide, or temple mentor.
- Cultural Role: Seen as respected but still junior. Many fail to advance beyond this stage.

==============================
🔹 4. VEILWALKER
==============================
- Description: Advanced practitioner. Can cast spells under duress, maintain Veil discipline, and participate in joint rituals.
- Signs: Efficient spellcasting, creation of magical tools or anchors, ability to teach Veilbound students.
- Often serves as scouts, battlefield tacticians, ritual support, or town guardians.
- Required: To pass the Council’s Veil Proficiency Trial for recognition.

==============================
🔹 5. MAGE
==============================
- Description: Fully trained, licensed, and sanctioned by the Council of the Veil.
- Rights: May participate in or conduct sacred rituals, lead Veil-based research, serve on magical courts.
- Responsibilities: Must follow the laws of magical use. Bound by oath not to misuse the Veil.
- Subtitles: “Temple Mage,” “Battle Mage,” “Ritual Conductor,” etc., depending on specialty.

==============================
⚠️ NOTES
==============================
• Beyond Mage: Titles such as Archmage, Veilwarden, or Conductor of the Circle exist, but are reserved for elite council members or sacred orders.

• Soulshape and Timeweave users are often initiated differently due to the risk and rarity of their affinities.

• Use of Dark Affinities disqualifies one from official ranks and marks them as a Veil Criminal.
