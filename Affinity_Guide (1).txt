AFFINITY GUIDE – VEILBOUND WORLD

⚖️ AFFINITY SYSTEM OVERVIEW
Every person is born with a dormant magical potential known as a Veilseed. This seed can develop into one of many magical Affinities — elemental or spiritual resonances that shape how a person interacts with the Veil (the source of all magic). Affinities are unchangeable once manifested.

==============================
🌟 COMMON AFFINITIES (1 in 20 people)
==============================
• Embercraft – 🔥
  - Gender: Masculine
  - Description: Affinity with fire and heat. Users can ignite small flames, shape and hurl fire, or heat objects to extreme temperatures. Used in warfare, forging, and intimidation.
  - Limitations: Cannot create fire without a source. Intense casting can cause dehydration or burn-out.
  
• Stonecall – 🪨
  - Gender: Masculine
  - Description: Earth and mineral manipulation. Allows creation of walls, weapon enhancements, mining control. Strong in siege defense or heavy labor.
  - Limitations: Requires physical contact with terrain. Slower casting time. Vulnerable to disruption.

• Windsway – 💨
  - Gender: Masculine
  - Description: Control over wind and air pressure. Enables ranged attacks, voice projection, wind boosts for speed or jumping.
  - Limitations: Ineffective in still or sealed environments. Casting is disrupted by sudden noise or physical attacks.

• Watersoul – 💧
  - Gender: Feminine
  - Description: Affinity with water in all forms. Supports minor healing, temperature control, and hydration rituals. Useful in agriculture and seafaring.
  - Limitations: Requires moisture nearby. Extended use drains stamina rapidly.

==============================
🌿 UNCOMMON AFFINITIES (1 in 200 people)
==============================
• Greentongue – 🌱
  - Gender: Feminine
  - Description: Control over plant life. Grow, accelerate, or communicate with flora. Used in healing, farming, or wilderness defense.
  - Limitations: Slow to manifest; requires sunlight and soil. Casting can overgrow or strangle if not guided carefully.

• Beastmark – 🐾
  - Gender: Neutral (slightly masculine)
  - Description: Deep empathy with animals. Communicate with or direct creatures over short distances. Used by scouts or breeders.
  - Limitations: Cannot force control. Strong-willed animals can resist. Weaker in cities or magical dead zones.

• Ironmind – 🧠
  - Gender: Masculine
  - Description: Mental defense, focus, and resistance to suggestion or illusion. Some develop minor telepathy or memory projection.
  - Limitations: Strains cognition over time. Can lead to emotional numbness if overused.

==============================
✨ RARE AFFINITIES (1 in 2,000 people)
==============================
• Heartsense – 💖
  - Gender: Feminine
  - Description: Empathy and healing. Read emotions, soothe pain, and help mend emotional or minor physical trauma.
  - Limitations: Physically and emotionally exhausting. Dangerous if used during emotional instability.

• Veilpierce – 👻
  - Gender: Neutral
  - Description: Contact with spirits and echoes of the dead. Can guide souls, receive visions, or hear truth through the Veil.
  - Limitations: Risk of madness, possession, or attracting malevolent entities.

• Mirrorbind – 🪞
  - Gender: Feminine
  - Description: Creates a magical soul link between two people. Used in royal bonding rituals and Veilbound pairings.
  - Limitations: Must be mutual and emotional. Can backfire if one soul rejects the other.

• Stillmark – 🕊️
  - Gender: Feminine
  - Description: Projection of calm. Suppresses emotion, stabilizes Veilflow. Used to calm crowds or anchor rituals.
  - Limitations: Self-casting may dull emotions permanently. Prolonged use can cause emotional burnout.

==============================
🕯️ FORBIDDEN / MYTHIC AFFINITIES (1 in 100,000+)
==============================
• Soulshape – 🌀
  - Gender: Neutral
  - Description: Rewrites physical form in line with spiritual identity. Used in gender transformation, healing, or age shifting.
  - Limitations: Only legal under divine cause. Requires 100+ Soulshapers. Failure causes soul rupture.

• Chronoken – ⏳
  - Gender: Neutral
  - Description: Perception of time echoes. View past/future fragments. Used by seers and prophetic temples.
  - Limitations: Memory loss, time disorientation. Cannot change fate—only glimpse it.

• Deathroot – ☠️
  - Gender: Forbidden
  - Description: Transfer of life energy from one being to another. Used to revive or destroy with sacrifice.
  - Limitations: Someone always dies. Corrupts bloodlines.

• Mindlock – 🧩
  - Gender: Forbidden
  - Description: Control thoughts or erase memories. Used in blackmail, interrogation, or dominion cults.
  - Limitations: Caster loses personality pieces. Victims suffer mental collapse.

• Veilshard – 🔮
  - Gender: Forbidden
  - Description: Shatter or bind a soul. Used in soul-curses, forbidden bindings.
  - Limitations: High chance of backlash. Creates hollow or broken people.

• Ashcall – 🔥💀
  - Gender: Forbidden
  - Description: Final spell cast at the cost of life. Total immolation, magical wildfire.
  - Limitations: Always fatal. Damages land and legacy. No known survivors.

==============================
🎭 SOCIETAL IMPACT OF GENDERED AFFINITIES
• Masculine Affinity in women = Often feared, seen as “unfeminine” or cursed.
• Feminine Affinity in men = Viewed as weak, inappropriate, or disgraceful in noble houses.
• Cross-Gender Affinity (CGA) = Acceptable among commoners. Taboo in noble circles.

==============================
🛡️ FINAL NOTES
• Affinities are fluid until they manifest (typically between ages 10–18).
• Early affinity tests are vague, unreliable, and often political.
• Trauma, emotion, or crisis can trigger manifestation.
• Forbidden affinities are recorded and hunted by the Council.
