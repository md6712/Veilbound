VEIL ATTUNEMENT RANKS – VEILBOUND WORLD (REVISED STRUCTURE)

🌫️ OVERVIEW
This classification is used across the known kingdoms and is recognized by the Council of the Veil. It defines a mage’s legal authority, level of training, and permitted use of magic. These ranks are used in courts, battlefields, temples, and council records.

==============================
1. VEILMARKED
==============================
- Description: Individuals who show signs of a dormant affinity but have not yet manifested it.
- Traits: Minor aura fluctuations, prophetic dreams, attraction to elemental sources.
- Legal Standing: No authority. Watched, sometimes tested — especially in noble families.
- Role: Observed, prepared, and sometimes sheltered or suppressed.
- Notes: Many Veilmarked never advance. Early tests are imprecise and often political.

==============================
2. VEILSPARK
==============================
- Description: A person who has manifested an affinity but has no formal training.
- Traits: Instinctive or erratic casting, often triggered by stress or trauma.
- Legal Standing: Registered but unlicensed. Can assist trained mages but not act independently.
- Role: Apprentices, battlefield aides, supervised temple assistants.
- Notes: This is where most magical training begins. Many stall here for years.

==============================
3. VEILCAST
==============================
- Description: A licensed and trained mage. Can cast without oversight.
- Traits: Competent in ritual work, combat casting, or healing. Affinity is fully stabilized.
- Legal Standing: Full authority to use magic under Council law.
- Role: Court mages, military casters, temple healers, magical engineers.
- Notes: Promotion to Veilcast requires Council examination and an oath of restraint.

==============================
4. VEILWARDEN
==============================
- Description: A master mage recognized by the Council. Commands deep authority and ritual leadership.
- Traits: Can coordinate group casting, train others, and act as legal arbiters in magical disputes.
- Legal Standing: Authorized to enforce magical law and lead council-licensed rituals.
- Role: Arch-healers, affinity instructors, ritual commanders.
- Notes: Veilwardens are feared and respected. They often wear the sigil of their Affinity openly.

==============================
5. VEILMAGE
==============================
- Description: A living legend. One who has survived, stabilized, or anchored mythic-level magic (e.g., Soulshape or Ashcall).
- Traits: Vast knowledge, ritual leadership, prophetic aura. Often tied to the fate of kingdoms.
- Legal Standing: Answer only to the Inviolate Court. Their word is law in ritual matters.
- Role: High Council members, mythic casters, living relics of the Veil.
- Notes: Fewer than a dozen Veilmages exist in the known world at any time.

==============================
⚠️ ADDITIONAL NOTES
==============================
• This scale is based on capability, not just power. Veilmages may not fight — but they shape nations.
• Possession of a Dark Affinity bars one from this hierarchy.
• Titles like “Conductor,” “Veilblade,” or “Whispered” may exist within sub-orders, but do not replace these ranks.
