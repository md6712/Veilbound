AFFINITY GUIDE – VEILBOUND WORLD

⚖️ AFFINITY SYSTEM OVERVIEW
Every person is born with a dormant magical potential known as a Veilseed. This seed can develop into one of many magical Affinities — elemental or spiritual resonances that shape how a person interacts with the Veil (the source of all magic). Affinities are unchangeable once manifested.

------------------------------
🌟 COMMON AFFINITIES (1 in 20 people)
• Embercraft – Fire control and heat manipulation. 🔥
  - Gender Association: Masculine
• Stonecall – Earth shaping, mineral and structure resonance. 🪨
  - Gender Association: Masculine
• Windsway – Wind movement, speed boosts, air shaping. 💨
  - Gender Association: Masculine
• Watersoul – Water manipulation, minor healing. 💧
  - Gender Association: Feminine

------------------------------
🌿 UNCOMMON AFFINITIES (1 in 200 people)
• Greentongue – Control of plant life, herbal manipulation. 🌱
  - Gender Association: Feminine
• Beastmark – Animal empathy or influence. 🐾
  - Gender Association: Neutral (slight masculine lean)
• Ironmind – Mental resilience, minor telepathy. 🧠
  - Gender Association: Masculine

------------------------------
✨ RARE AFFINITIES (1 in 2,000 people)
• Heartsense – Emotional healing, sensing, calming. 💖
  - Gender Association: Feminine
• Veilpierce – Communicating with spirits or the dead. 👻
  - Gender Association: Neutral (often feared)
• Mirrorbind – Soul-linking, used in bonded transformations. 🪞
  - Gender Association: Feminine
• Stillmark – Emotional suppression and calm projection. 🕊️
  - Gender Association: Feminine

------------------------------
🕯️ FORBIDDEN / MYTHIC AFFINITIES (1 in 100,000+)
• Soulshape – Transformation of body and soul. 🌀
  - Gender Association: Neutral (used in identity rites)
• Chronoken – Manipulating glimpses of time. ⏳
  - Gender Association: Neutral
• Deathroot – Life-for-life healing or harm. ☠️
  - Gender Association: Forbidden
• Mindlock – Thought/memory domination. 🧩
  - Gender Association: Forbidden
• Veilshard – Soul damage and fragmentation. 🔮
  - Gender Association: Forbidden
• Ashcall – Self-immolation to create pure magical destruction. 🔥💀
  - Gender Association: Forbidden

------------------------------
🎭 SOCIETAL IMPACT OF GENDERED AFFINITIES
• Masculine Affinity in women = Often feared, seen as "unfeminine"
• Feminine Affinity in men = Social stigma, especially among nobility
• Cross-Gender Affinity (CGA) = Taboo in noble circles, often leads to exile or disowning

------------------------------
🛡️ FINAL NOTES
• Affinities are fluid before manifestation.
• Early tests are vague, culturally influential, but not reliable.
• Emotional trauma or stress often triggers the final manifestation.
• Stronger magic requires group rituals (United Casting).
• Every affinity comes with cost — fatigue, Veil Burn, or instability.
