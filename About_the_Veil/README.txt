ABOUT THE VEIL - ORGANIZED LORE MAP

The Veil is the source of magic, soul pressure, transformation, and sacred law in Veilbound. Every person is born with a dormant Veilseed. When that seed awakens, it manifests as a fixed Affinity.

This folder separates three concepts:

1. Affinities: specific magical resonances such as Soulshape, Stillmark, Windsway, and Greentongue.
2. Attunement Ranks: Council-recognized levels of training and legal authority.
3. The Sacred Twelve Veils: spiritual principles used in oaths, rituals, and transformation lore. They are not spell types.

Canon notes:
- Soulshape can permanently reshape body and spirit.
- Full gender transformation requires consent, Council oversight, and more than one hundred Soulshapers.
- Kaelira and Rinya are fully feminine after transformation: body, voice, social role, presentation, and spiritual harmony all shift into womanhood.
- A Veilbound bond lets two people share Veilflow, stabilize one another, and recover from Veil strain.
