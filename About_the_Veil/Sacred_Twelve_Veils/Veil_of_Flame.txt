Veil of Flame

Type: Sacred principle, not an affinity

Meaning:
Will, courage, purification, anger, passion, and destructive resolve.

Ritual use:
Invoked in oaths, transformation rites, funerals, royal vows, and Council language when the speaker calls on a force larger than ordinary magic.

Story connection:
The Twelve Veils together describe the whole shape of life and soul. Kaelira and Rinya's transformation especially touches Change, Sacrifice, Blood, Silence, Emotion, and Truth.
