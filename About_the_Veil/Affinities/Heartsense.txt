Heartsense

Rarity: Rare
Gendered resonance: Feminine
Category: Affinity

Core function:
Empathic healing and emotional perception. Reads pain, soothes distress, and supports recovery.

Known limitations:
Emotionally exhausting and dangerous during instability.

Story use:
Mage Miranda is a Heartsense. She contrasts it with Stillmark.

Societal note:
Gendered affinities affect noble reputation. Masculine affinities in women and feminine affinities in men are politically sensitive, while neutral affinities can cross expected boundaries.
