Timeweave

Rarity: Sacred
Gendered resonance: Neutral
Category: Affinity

Core function:
Perception of time echoes, past/future fragments, prophetic impressions, and fate-patterns.

Known limitations:
Can cause memory loss and disorientation. Cannot change fate directly.

Story use:
Worldbuilding affinity with no major confirmed current wielder.

Societal note:
Gendered affinities affect noble reputation. Masculine affinities in women and feminine affinities in men are politically sensitive, while neutral affinities can cross expected boundaries.
