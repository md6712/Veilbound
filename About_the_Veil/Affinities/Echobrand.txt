Echobrand

Rarity: Rare
Gendered resonance: Masculine
Category: Affinity

Core function:
Summons ancestral weapons, inherited combat forms, or martial echoes.

Known limitations:
One echo at a time. Ghostburn can cause identity confusion.

Story use:
Therrin is marked as Echobrand in the merged list, though story confirmation is pending.

Societal note:
Gendered affinities affect noble reputation. Masculine affinities in women and feminine affinities in men are politically sensitive, while neutral affinities can cross expected boundaries.
