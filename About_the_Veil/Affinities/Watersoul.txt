Watersoul

Rarity: Common
Gendered resonance: Feminine
Category: Affinity

Core function:
Water, moisture, hydration rites, minor healing support, and temperature balance.

Known limitations:
Needs nearby water or moisture. Extended use drains stamina.

Story use:
Listed as one possible early test result for Rynn, but not his final affinity.

Societal note:
Gendered affinities affect noble reputation. Masculine affinities in women and feminine affinities in men are politically sensitive, while neutral affinities can cross expected boundaries.
