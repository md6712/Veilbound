Stillmark

Rarity: Rare
Gendered resonance: Feminine
Category: Affinity

Core function:
Projection of calm, suppression of panic, grief, rage, and emotional volatility. Stabilizes Veilflow.

Known limitations:
Self-use risks numbness, feedback loops, permanent Veil loss, or death before high training.

Story use:
Rinya manifests Stillmark. Lady Maerel also has Stillmark.

Societal note:
Gendered affinities affect noble reputation. Masculine affinities in women and feminine affinities in men are politically sensitive, while neutral affinities can cross expected boundaries.
