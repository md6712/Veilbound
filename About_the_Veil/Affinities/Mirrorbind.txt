Mirrorbind

Rarity: Rare
Gendered resonance: Feminine
Category: Affinity

Core function:
Soul-linking, mutual bonds, royal bonding rites, and Veilbound-style pairings.

Known limitations:
Must be mutual and emotionally compatible. Rejection can damage both souls.

Story use:
Related to the Kaelira/Rinya bond, though their bond is ritual-prepared.

Societal note:
Gendered affinities affect noble reputation. Masculine affinities in women and feminine affinities in men are politically sensitive, while neutral affinities can cross expected boundaries.
