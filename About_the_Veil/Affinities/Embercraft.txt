Embercraft

Rarity: Common
Gendered resonance: Masculine
Category: Affinity

Core function:
Fire and heat. Users shape existing flame, heat metal, weaken weapon grips, and hurl controlled fire.

Known limitations:
Requires flame or heat source. Heavy use causes thirst, exhaustion, burns, and Veil burnout.

Story use:
Joval is confirmed with Embercraft in the candidate group and escort scenes.

Societal note:
Gendered affinities affect noble reputation. Masculine affinities in women and feminine affinities in men are politically sensitive, while neutral affinities can cross expected boundaries.
