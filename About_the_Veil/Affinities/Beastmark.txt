Beastmark

Rarity: Uncommon
Gendered resonance: Neutral, slightly masculine
Category: Affinity

Core function:
Animal empathy and short-range communication with beasts.

Known limitations:
Cannot override a strong-willed animal. Weaker in cities and dead zones.

Story use:
Worldbuilding affinity with no major confirmed current wielder.

Societal note:
Gendered affinities affect noble reputation. Masculine affinities in women and feminine affinities in men are politically sensitive, while neutral affinities can cross expected boundaries.
