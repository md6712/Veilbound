Windsway

Rarity: Common
Gendered resonance: Masculine
Category: Affinity

Core function:
Wind, air pressure, movement, voice projection, and speed support.

Known limitations:
Weak in sealed or still environments. Disrupted breath, injury, or sudden noise can break focus.

Story use:
Cale manifests Windsway during the duel. Marek also wields Windsway.

Societal note:
Gendered affinities affect noble reputation. Masculine affinities in women and feminine affinities in men are politically sensitive, while neutral affinities can cross expected boundaries.
