Mindlock

Rarity: Dark
Gendered resonance: Forbidden
Category: Affinity

Core function:
Controls thoughts, erases memories, and breaks mental autonomy.

Known limitations:
Caster loses pieces of self. Victims suffer collapse.

Story use:
Named by Miranda as dark magic.

Societal note:
Gendered affinities affect noble reputation. Masculine affinities in women and feminine affinities in men are politically sensitive, while neutral affinities can cross expected boundaries.
