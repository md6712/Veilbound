Deathroot

Rarity: Dark
Gendered resonance: Forbidden
Category: Affinity

Core function:
Transfers life energy from one being to another; can revive or destroy through sacrifice.

Known limitations:
Someone always pays. Corrupts bloodlines and is hunted by the Council.

Story use:
Named by Miranda as dark magic.

Societal note:
Gendered affinities affect noble reputation. Masculine affinities in women and feminine affinities in men are politically sensitive, while neutral affinities can cross expected boundaries.
