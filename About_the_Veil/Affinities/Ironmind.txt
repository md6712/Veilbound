Ironmind

Rarity: Uncommon
Gendered resonance: Masculine
Category: Affinity

Core function:
Mental defense, focus, resistance to suggestion, and possible advanced memory projection.

Known limitations:
Can numb emotion or strain cognition when overused.

Story use:
Prince Eryndor and Prince Aelyon/Judew are listed with Ironmind.

Societal note:
Gendered affinities affect noble reputation. Masculine affinities in women and feminine affinities in men are politically sensitive, while neutral affinities can cross expected boundaries.
