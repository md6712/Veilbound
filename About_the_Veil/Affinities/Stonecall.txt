Stonecall

Rarity: Common
Gendered resonance: Masculine
Category: Affinity

Core function:
Earth, stone, and mineral manipulation for walls, siege defense, mining, and weapon reinforcement.

Known limitations:
Requires contact with earth or stone. Casting is slower and can be disrupted.

Story use:
Deren manifests Stonecall and is disqualified from Veilbound eligibility.

Societal note:
Gendered affinities affect noble reputation. Masculine affinities in women and feminine affinities in men are politically sensitive, while neutral affinities can cross expected boundaries.
