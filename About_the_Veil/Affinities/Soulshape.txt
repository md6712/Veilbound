Soulshape

Rarity: Rare to Sacred
Gendered resonance: Neutral
Category: Affinity

Core function:
Reshapes the bond between soul and body. Used for scars, features, healing, gender transformation, and deep body rewriting.

Known limitations:
Strictly regulated. Full transformation requires consent, Council oversight, and more than one hundred Soulshapers.

Story use:
Kaelira is a Soulshaper. Mage Vistan leads the ritual. Kaelen becomes Kaelira and Rynn becomes Rinya.

Societal note:
Gendered affinities affect noble reputation. Masculine affinities in women and feminine affinities in men are politically sensitive, while neutral affinities can cross expected boundaries.
