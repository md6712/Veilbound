Greentongue

Rarity: Uncommon
Gendered resonance: Feminine
Category: Affinity

Core function:
Plant growth, plant communication, agriculture, healing gardens, and wilderness defense.

Known limitations:
Needs living plants, soil, or sunlight. Poor control can cause dangerous overgrowth.

Story use:
Serenya Maeren and Lady Elsin are listed with Greentongue.

Societal note:
Gendered affinities affect noble reputation. Masculine affinities in women and feminine affinities in men are politically sensitive, while neutral affinities can cross expected boundaries.
